BLINK TRAINING SYSTEM
Bite-sized business training on mobile devices

What is Blink Training?

Blink combines the content of e-learning and training sims with the fun, feedback and friends of mobile gaming. And Blink Training is available 24/7 on your employees' mobile devices.

Training becomes a part of your employees' gaming and entertainment lifestyle.

How does Blink work?

We break your content into small, easy-to-grasp units so your employees can conveniently train for even just a few minutes at a time – in or out of the office. We add simple gaming activities, points and levels to engage and motivate your employees. We track your employees' progress and keep content in the loop until it is mastered. We give HR full visibility to review any employees' progress at any time.

How does Blink compare to traditional e-learning?

More Engaging

Playful training in small chunks provides autonomy and control

More Efficient

Short bursts fill in small breaks in and out of the office which means less time is 'lost' to training

More Effective

Training blends into the employee's media and gaming lifestyle transforming it from a chore to another experience of challenge and triumph

Less Expensive

Blink courses are less expensive to create, update and support,and require no office computers

Fosters Self-Improvement

Learners in control, friendly competition, and frequent victories fosters a community of self determined personal improvement
